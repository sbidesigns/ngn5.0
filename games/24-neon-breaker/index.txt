NGN4 GAME #24: NEON BREAKER
════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TITLE:    Neon Breaker
GENRE:    Brick Breaker / Breakout
LORE:     "The Neon Barrier seals an ancient threat. Shatter it layer by layer before it's too late."

FEATURES:
  - 30 levels with procedural brick layouts
  - 8 brick types: Normal, Tough (2HP), Metal (3HP), Explosive (chain), Indestructible, Power-up, Multi-ball, Laser
  - 10 power-ups: Wide paddle, Multi-ball, Laser, Fireball, Slow, Sticky, Extra Life, Coin Rain, Shield, Magnet
  - Boss bricks every 10 levels (indestructible walls with weak spots)
  - Combo scoring system
  - Particle effects on brick destruction
  - Level editor with click/right-click brick placement
  - Mouse and touch controls

CONTROLS:
  - Mouse/Touch to move paddle
  - Click to launch ball
  - Right-click in editor to cycle brick types

REWARDS:
  - +1 coin per brick broken
  - +5 coins per power-up collected
  - +50 coins per level clear
  - Bonus coins for remaining lives
  - Continue costs 5 gems
  - All rewards saved to ngn4_rewards

SCREENS:
  - Main menu → How to play → Game (canvas + HUD)
  - Level complete results → Game over (with continue/retry)
  - Level editor (custom level creation)
  - Rewarded ad between boss level sets

TECH:
  - Canvas 2D rendering with glow effects
  - Web Audio oscillators for sound
  - Particle system
  - localStorage for rewards
  - Touch support
  - requestAnimationFrame game loop
