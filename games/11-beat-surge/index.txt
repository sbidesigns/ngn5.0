NGN4 Game Engine - Game #11
Title: Beat Surge
Genre: Rhythm / Music
Version: 1.0

STORY:
In a world where music powers technology, you are a Beat Runner — channeling rhythms to keep the city alive. Hit notes in time with the beat to power the city's systems. Miss too many and the lights go out forever.

GAMEPLAY:
- Notes fall from the top of the screen in 4 lanes
- Press D, F, J, K to hit notes as they reach the hit line
- Timing matters: Perfect (18px), Great (32px), Good (50px), Miss
- Build combos for score multipliers (up to 8x)
- Health depletes on misses; song fails at 0 health

FEATURES:
- 10 songs with BPM from 80-180
- 3 difficulty levels (Easy/Medium/Hard)
- Combo system with multipliers (1x-8x)
- Star rating (1-3 stars per song)
- Particle effects on note hits
- Beat pulse visual effects
- Song unlock system with coins
- Accuracy tracking per song

CONTROLS:
D - Lane 1 (Red)
F - Lane 2 (Green)
J - Lane 3 (Blue)
K - Lane 4 (Yellow)

REWARDS:
- Perfect hit: 10 pts × multiplier
- Great hit: 7 pts × multiplier
- Good hit: 3 pts × multiplier
- Completion bonus: 50 coins per star earned
- Coins convert from score (score / 10)

AD INTEGRATION:
- Rewarded ad between songs
- Rewarded ad to revive on song failure
