# NGN4 Game: Crystal Heights
## 3D Platformer

### Overview
The Crystal Spires hold ancient power. Climb ever higher to reach the Apex Crystal before the Shadow Council claims it.

### Gameplay
- 3D platform climbing using Babylon.js
- Collect crystals and reach the portal at each level's end
- 8 levels with increasing complexity

### Controls
- W/↑/SPACE: Jump (Double Jump supported!)
- A/D or ←→: Move left/right
- S/↓: Fast fall
- Mobile: On-screen directional buttons and jump

### Features
- Double jump mechanic
- Moving platforms (from Level 2+)
- Bounce pads (from Level 4+)
- Enemy obstacles - patrolling shadows (from Level 3+)
- 3 Crystal types:
  - Blue: 100 points + 10 coins
  - Red: 50 points + health restore
  - Gold: 500 points + 50 coins
- Lives system (3 hearts, max 5)
- Level select screen with star ratings

### Level Progression
1. Foundation Spire - Learn the basics
2. Crystal Garden - Moving platforms
3. Shadow Corridor - Enemies patrol
4. Bounce Tower - Launch skyward
5. The Gauntlet - Everything combined
6. Frozen Ascent - Slippery platforms
7. Shadow Council - The council awaits
8. Apex Crystal - The final climb

### Rewards
- Crystals convert to coins
- Star rating (1-3) based on completion time
- Star bonuses: 1★=0, 2★=+100, 3★=+200 coins
- Level completion bonus

### Ad Integration
- Interstitial ads between levels
- Rewarded ad for extra life on level complete
- Rewarded ad for revive on game over

### Technical
- Babylon.js CDN for 3D rendering
- Procedural level generation
- GlowLayer for neon effects
- localStorage for star progress
- Mobile touch controls
