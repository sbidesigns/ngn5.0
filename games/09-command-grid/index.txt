NGN4 Game 9: Command Grid
===========================
Genre: Turn-Based Strategy
Engine: Canvas 2D
Controls: Click to select units, click to move, click to attack

Story:
Two rival commanders vie for control of the Grid Sector. Deploy units, capture territory, outmaneuver your opponent.

Features:
- 5 unit types: Scout, Soldier, Heavy, Sniper, Medic
- 8 campaign missions with unique maps and objectives
- Fog of war system
- Terrain types: Plains, Forest, Mountain, Building, Water
- Capture 3 of 5 control points to win each mission
- Counter-attack system when enemies survive
- AI opponent with tactical decision-making
- Mission briefings and results with coin rewards
- Flawless victory bonus
- NGN4 coin rewards and ad integration between missions

File: index.html, style.css, game.js
