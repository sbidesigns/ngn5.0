NGN4 GAME #21: DATA EMPIRE
════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TITLE:    Data Empire
GENRE:    Incremental / Idle Tycoon
LORE:     "Start with a garage and a dream. Build a tech empire that dominates the global market."

FEATURES:
  - 8 Business types: Startup → App → Platform → Cloud → AI → Quantum → Space → Galactic
  - Incremental click-to-earn mechanics with passive income
  - Hire managers to automate revenue collection
  - Prestige system — reset for permanent multiplier boost and gems
  - Dynamic market events (boom/bust/viral PR/innovation)
  - Stock market mini-game with real-time price chart
  - 12 milestone achievements with gem rewards
  - Rewarded ad system (2x income for 30 seconds)
  - Auto-save every 10 seconds
  - Dark neon theme, monospace/Orbitron fonts

CONTROLS:
  - Click "Generate Revenue" to earn money manually
  - Buy businesses to unlock passive income streams
  - Hire managers for automatic collection
  - Prestige to earn gems and permanent multiplier
  - Trade stocks in the stock market mini-game

REWARDS:
  - Income converts to coins (1 coin per $1000 earned)
  - Prestige earns gems (based on total earned)
  - Achievements grant +5 gems each
  - Coins and gems saved to ngn4_rewards (localStorage)

SCREENS:
  - Main menu → How to play → Game (business panel + actions)
  - Stock market overlay with real-time chart
  - Prestige confirmation screen
  - Rewarded ad mockup (5s timer → 2x income boost)

TECH:
  - Pure HTML5 Canvas + DOM, no frameworks
  - Web Audio oscillators for sound effects
  - localStorage for save/persistence and rewards
  - requestAnimationFrame game loop
  - Responsive layout
