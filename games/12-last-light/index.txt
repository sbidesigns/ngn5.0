NGN4 Game Engine - Game #12
Title: Last Light
Genre: Survival
Version: 1.0

STORY:
The sun died 47 days ago. You are the Keeper of the Last Light — maintain the flame while creatures of darkness close in. Survive 10 days and nights to keep the last beacon burning.

GAMEPLAY:
- DAY PHASE (3 minutes): Explore the world, gather resources (wood, food, scrap)
- NIGHT PHASE (2 minutes): Return to beacon, defend against waves of dark creatures
- Craft items during either phase to help survival
- Survive all 10 days to win

FEATURES:
- Day/night cycle with visual transitions
- 3 resource types scattered across the map
- 4 craftable items: Torch (wards enemies), Wall (barrier), Turret (auto-attacks), Health Pack
- 5 enemy types with scaling difficulty per day
- Stamina system for sprinting
- Minimap showing resources, enemies, and structures
- Particle effects for gathering and combat
- 10 days of increasing challenge

CONTROLS:
WASD - Move
Shift - Sprint (uses stamina)
E/Space - Attack nearby enemies
1 - Craft Torch (2 wood)
2 - Craft Wall (3 wood + 2 scrap)
3 - Craft Turret (5 scrap + 2 wood)
4 - Craft Health Pack (3 food)

REWARDS:
+5 coins per resource gathered
+20 coins per enemy killed (scales by type)
+100 coins per night survived
+500 coins bonus for completing all 10 nights

AD INTEGRATION:
- Rewarded ad after victory screen
- Between days (extra resources opportunity)
