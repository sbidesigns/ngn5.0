NGN4 Game 8: Street Neon
=========================
Genre: Beat 'em Up
Engine: Canvas 2D
Controls: Arrow Keys to move, Z = Attack, X = Jump, C = Special (when meter full)

Story:
The Neon Syndicate controls the streets. You're an ex-enforcer with a score to settle. Fight through 6 stages of neon-lit streets to defeat the Syndicate.

Features:
- 3 playable characters: Brawler, Ninja, Tech
- Combo chain attack system
- 6 stages with unique backgrounds and enemy waves
- Boss fights on each stage
- Special meter with unique abilities per character
- Health and special pickups
- Combo counter with bonus rewards
- Parallax scrolling backgrounds with neon aesthetics
- NGN4 coin rewards and ad integration between stages

File: index.html, style.css, game.js
