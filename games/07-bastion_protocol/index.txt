NGN4 Game 7: Bastion Protocol
=============================
Genre: Tower Defense
Engine: Canvas 2D
Controls: Click tower buttons to select, click grid to place. Click placed towers for upgrade/sell.

Story:
The AI uprising has begun. Deploy defense towers along the data streams to protect the last human server.

Features:
- 5 tower types: Cannon, Laser, Frost, Tesla, Missile
- 6 enemy types: Drone, Speeder, Tank, Shield, Heavy, Boss
- 15 waves per level with increasing difficulty
- Tower upgrade system (3 levels per tower)
- Sell towers for 60% refund
- Lives system (20 lives)
- Speed control (1x, 2x, 3x)
- Particle effects and visual feedback
- Tower range preview on placement
- NGN4 coin rewards and ad integration between levels

File: index.html, style.css, game.js
