NGN4 Game #35: Ring Champion
Genre: Sports / Boxing
Story: From underground clubs to the World Championship — fight your way through 12 opponents to become the undisputed champion.
Features: 12 unique opponents with different styles (aggressive, defensive, counter, balanced), stamina system, health bars, 3-round fights (90s), combo system, knockdown, training mini-games, stat upgrades, championship progression
