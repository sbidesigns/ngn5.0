CIPHER QUEST - NGN4 Game #40
Puzzle Adventure Game

STORY:
An ancient cipher holds the key to a lost civilization. Solve puzzles across 10 temples to unlock the final secret.

CONTROLS:
Mouse/Click - Interact with puzzles
Each level has unique puzzle mechanics

FEATURES:
10 levels with different puzzle types:
  1. Pattern Sequence (repeat the pattern)
  2. Sliding Puzzle (3x3)
  3. Logic Gates (AND/OR/NOT wiring)
  4. Word Cipher (decode encrypted message)
  5. Pipe Connection (connect start to end)
  6. Light Reflection (angle mirrors to hit target)
  7. Color Mixing (combine to match target)
  8. Simon Says (increasing sequence)
  9. Maze with Keys
  10. Final Boss: combination of all types

- Hint system
- Star rating (1-3 stars per level)
- Collectible lore fragments in each level
- Progressive difficulty

REWARDS:
+100 per level complete
+25 per hint not used
+50 per lore fragment
Bonus for all stars

AD INTEGRATION:
Rewarded ad between levels for hint
