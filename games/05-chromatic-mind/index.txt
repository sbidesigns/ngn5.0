# NGN4 Game: Chromatic Mind
## 3D Match-3 Puzzle

### Overview
The Chromatic Nexus is collapsing. Match the resonance frequencies to stabilize reality before dimensions merge.

### Gameplay
- 3D match-3 puzzle on an 8x8 grid using Babylon.js
- Click two adjacent cubes to swap them
- Match 3+ same-colored cubes to score points
- 10 levels with increasing difficulty and score targets

### Controls
- Click: Select cube
- Click adjacent cube: Swap
- Mobile: Tap to select, tap adjacent to swap

### Cube Colors (6 types)
1. Red
2. Blue
3. Green
4. Yellow
5. Purple
6. Cyan

### Special Blocks
- Match 4 in a row → Lightning (clears entire row)
- Match 5+ in a row → Rainbow/Wildcard
- Bomb blocks: Clear 3x3 area when matched

### Features
- Combo multiplier system (up to 10x)
- Cascade system: cubes fall and chain reactions occur
- Move limits per level
- Star rating (1-3) based on score achieved
- Level select screen
- Auto-shuffle when no moves available
- Hint system (watch ad for hint on next level)

### Level Progression
1. First Resonance - 500 pts, 30 moves, 4 colors
2. Color Shift - 800 pts, 28 moves, 5 colors
3. Deep Resonance - 1200 pts, 26 moves, 5 colors
4. Prismatic - 1500 pts, 25 moves, 6 colors
5. Chaos Theory - 2000 pts, 25 moves, 6 colors
6. Dimension Rift - 2500 pts, 24 moves, 6 colors
7. Quantum Match - 3000 pts, 23 moves, 6 colors
8. Singularity - 3500 pts, 22 moves, 6 colors
9. Void Collapse - 4000 pts, 22 moves, 6 colors
10. Nexus Core - 5000 pts, 20 moves, 6 colors

### Rewards
- Points convert to coins (1pt = 0.1 coins)
- Star bonuses: 1★=+0, 2★=+50, 3★=+100 coins
- Level clear bonus

### Star Ratings
- 1 Star: Reach target score
- 2 Stars: Reach 1.5x target
- 3 Stars: Reach 2x target

### Ad Integration
- Interstitial ads between levels
- Rewarded ad for +5 extra moves on failure
- Rewarded ad for hint on level complete

### Technical
- Babylon.js CDN for 3D rendering
- GlowLayer for neon effects
- Smooth animation system for swaps and cascades
- Particle effects on matches
- localStorage for star progress
