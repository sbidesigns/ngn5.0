NGN4 GAME 28: SKY DRIFT
════════════════════════════════════════════════
Flappy-Style Game

STORY: You are a sky courier navigating the treacherous Neon Canyons. One tap to ascend, survive the gauntlet.

GAMEPLAY:
- Tap/click/spacebar to flap and ascend
- Avoid pipes and ground/ceiling
- Survive as long as possible for the highest score
- Collect coins between pipes for bonus rewards
- Grab power-ups for special abilities

FEATURES:
- Procedurally generated pipes with varying gap sizes
- 8 bird skins with unique visual effects
- 5 environments (Neon Canyon, Crystal Cave, Storm Clouds, Solar Flare, The Void)
- Medal system (Bronze/Silver/Gold/Platinum)
- Coin pickups between pipes
- Power-ups (Slow Motion, Shield)
- Daily challenge mode
- Increasing difficulty with score
- Particle effects and trail systems
- Progressive environment unlocks

SKINS:
- Classic (free), Phoenix (50), Ice Bird (100), Shadow (150)
- Neon (200), Golden (300), Cyber (500), Cosmic (750)

REWARDS:
- +1 coin per pipe passed
- +5 coins per coin collected
- +20 coins for new best distance
- Skins purchasable with coins

AD INTEGRATION:
- Death screen: Rewarded ad for continue from last position
- Environment unlock ads between stages

CONTROLS:
- Click/Tap/Space/ArrowUp to flap
- One input = one flap
