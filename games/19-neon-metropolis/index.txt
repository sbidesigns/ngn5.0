NGN4 Game #19: Neon Metropolis
==============================
Genre: Grid-Based City Builder
Story: As the newly elected Mayor of Neon District, transform this wasteland into a thriving cyberpunk metropolis.

Features:
- 15 building types (Residential, Commercial, Industrial, Power Plant, Solar Array, Police, Hospital, School, Park, Road, Fire Station, Stadium, Mall, Apartments, Research Lab)
- Resource management: Credits, Power, Population, Happiness
- Building upgrades (3 levels per building)
- Population growth based on happiness
- Tax income system based on population
- Random events (Crime Wave, Festival, Storm, Economic Boom, Pandemic, Tourism, Power Outage, Construction Boom)
- Achievement milestones (50/100/250/500/1000 population)
- Statistics dashboard with building breakdown
- Save/load game progress
- Construction discount events

Controls:
- Click building from panel, then click grid to place
- D: Toggle demolish mode
- U: Upgrade hovered building
- Space: Advance to next day

Rewards:
- Tax income = coins earned per day
- Milestone rewards: +50 to +1000 coins
- Population milestones tracked and rewarded

Ad Integration:
- Rewarded ad for instant building completion
- Rewarded ad for disaster relief
