NGN4 Game #20: Sunrise Acres
=============================
Genre: Top-Down Farming Simulator
Story: You inherited your grandfather's farm. Restore it to glory and become the champion of the Harvest Festival.

Features:
- 12 crop types: Wheat, Corn, Tomato, Carrot, Strawberry, Pumpkin, Potato, Lettuce, Melon, Grape, Cabbage, Snow Pea
- 6 animal types: Chicken, Cow, Pig, Sheep, Horse, Duck
- 4 seasons (Spring, Summer, Fall, Winter) with 30 days each
- Different crops available per season
- Market with fluctuating prices (buy seeds, sell produce)
- Tool system: Hoe, Water Can, Harvest, Plant Seeds, Pet Animals, Remove
- Tool upgrades (Better Watering Can, Better Hoe, Bigger Bag, Farm Expansion)
- NPC neighbor quest system (8 unique quests)
- Energy system with day/night cycle
- Animal product collection (Eggs, Milk, Truffles, Wool, Duck Eggs)
- Harvest Festival competition with Gold/Silver/Bronze tiers
- Save/load farm progress
- Visual crop growth stages (3 stages)
- Seasonal visual themes

Controls:
- 1-6: Select tool
- Click: Use selected tool on tile
- M: Open market
- N: Next day
- F: Enter Harvest Festival

Rewards:
- Sell crops and animal products for coins
- Festival awards: +30 to +500 coins based on tier
- Quest completion rewards: +150 to +400 coins
- Coins earned through market transactions

Ad Integration:
- Rewarded ad for instant crop growth
- Interstitial ad between seasons
- Rewarded ad for energy refill option
