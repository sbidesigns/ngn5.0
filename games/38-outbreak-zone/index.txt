OUTBREAK ZONE - NGN4 Game #38
Zombie Survival Shooter

STORY:
Day 1: The outbreak began. Day 47: You're still alive. Hold the line at the evacuation point until rescue arrives.

CONTROLS:
WASD - Move
Mouse - Aim
Click - Shoot
R - Reload
B - Build barricade
1-8 - Switch weapons

FEATURES:
- 20 waves of zombie hordes
- 6 zombie types: Walker, Runner, Tank, Spitter, Exploder, Boss
- 8 weapons: Pistol, Shotgun, SMG, Rifle, Sniper, Flamethrower, Rocket Launcher, Minigun
- Barricade building (wood, metal)
- Resource scavenging between waves
- Weapon shop
- Survivor recruitment (AI allies)
- Day/night cycle (harder at night)
- Supply drops

REWARDS:
+5 per zombie kill
+50 per wave survived
+20 per supply found
Weapons/barricades cost coins

AD INTEGRATION:
Rewarded ad between every 5 waves for supply crate
