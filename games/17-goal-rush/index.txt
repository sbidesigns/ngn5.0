NGN4 Game #17: Goal Rush
========================
Genre: Top-Down Arcade Soccer
Story: The Neon Cup is the ultimate prize. Lead your underdog team from the streets to the world stage.

Features:
- 5v5 top-down arcade soccer gameplay
- Player controls one character, switchable with E key
- AI teammates and opponents with intelligent positioning
- Power shot meter (hold Space)
- Pass system (Q key)
- Sprint/slide mechanic (Shift)
- 2-minute halves (2 halves per match)
- 8 tournament rounds with increasing difficulty
- Player stats: Speed, Shot, Pass, Defense
- Stat upgrades and shop items purchasable with coins
- Team uniform and stadium customization

Controls:
- WASD: Move player
- Space (hold): Charge power shot, release to kick
- Q: Pass to nearest teammate
- E: Switch controlled player
- Shift: Sprint boost

Rewards:
- +5 coins per goal scored
- +50 coins per match win
- +10 coins per assist
- +200 coins for tournament championship

Ad Integration:
- Interstitial ad between tournament matches
- Rewarded ad for temporary stat boost option
