SKY VIPERS - NGN4 Game #36
Air Combat Top-Down Shooter

STORY:
The Sky Vipers are the elite fighter squadron. Lead your wing against the rogue states threatening global peace.

CONTROLS:
WASD - Move aircraft
Mouse - Aim
Click/Space - Shoot
Scroll/1-3 - Switch weapons

FEATURES:
- 3 weapon types: Machine Gun, Missiles, Laser
- 8 enemy types: Fighters, Bombers, Helicopters, Drones, AA Turrets, Boss Ships
- 10 campaign missions with objectives
- Health/Armor system
- Weapon pickups
- Wingman AI ally
- Radar minimap
- Cloud cover visual effects
- Explosion effects

REWARDS:
+10 coins per kill
+50 coins per mission complete
+200 coins per boss kill
Weapon upgrades cost coins

AD INTEGRATION:
Rewarded ad between missions for full ammo/health refill
