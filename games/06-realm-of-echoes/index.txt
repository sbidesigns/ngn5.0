NGN4 Game 6: Realm of Echoes
=============================
Genre: RPG Adventure
Engine: Canvas 2D
Controls: WASD to move, click to interact, 1/2 for potions

Story:
The Realm of Echoes is fractured. Ancient spirits have gone mad, and only a Wanderer bonded to an Echo Spirit can restore balance. Descend through 5 dungeon floors, defeat enemies, find equipment, and face the Echo Dragon Lord.

Features:
- Procedural dungeon generation with rooms and corridors
- 4 enemy types: Slime, Skeleton, Dark Mage, Dragon
- Turn-based combat with Attack, Magic, Items, Flee
- XP/Leveling system with stat growth
- Equipment system: Sword, Shield, Armor
- Inventory: HP Potions, MP Potions
- 5 dungeon floors with boss on floor 5
- Minimap navigation
- Shop for equipment and potions
- NGN4 coin rewards and ad integration between floors

File: index.html, style.css, game.js
