NGN4 Game Engine - Game #15
Title: Shadow Veil
Genre: Stealth Action
Version: 1.0

STORY:
Infiltrate the Corporation's most secure facility. You are unseen. You are unstoppable. You are Shadow. Complete 10 levels of increasingly complex infiltration missions.

GAMEPLAY:
- Top-down stealth action
- Move with WASD, avoid guard sight cones
- Reach extraction point to complete each level
- Complete secondary objectives for bonus rewards
- Stay undetected for 3-star rating

FEATURES:
- 10 levels with increasing complexity
- Guard AI: Patrol, Investigate noise, Alert/Chase
- Hiding spots: Shadows, Lockers, Vents
- Distraction items: Coins, Noisemakers
- Security cameras with sweeping vision cones
- Locked doors requiring keycards
- Secondary objectives: Terminals, Documents, Prisoners
- Detection meter (fills when spotted, decays when hidden)
- Non-lethal takedowns
- 3-star rating based on detection count
- Gadget shop for purchasing equipment

GUARD AI STATES:
- PATROL: Follows waypoint path
- INVESTIGATE: Moves toward noise source, then returns to patrol
- ALERT: Chases player, triggers game over if close enough

CONTROLS:
WASD - Move
E - Interact (hide, pick up items, complete objectives)
T - Non-lethal takedown (requires gadget, must be near guard)
1 - Throw coin (distraction)
2 - Throw noisemaker (distraction)
ESC - Exit hiding spot

REWARDS:
+50 coins per level
+25 bonus for zero detection (3-star)
+10 per secondary objective
Gadget unlocks via coin shop

AD INTEGRATION:
- Rewarded ad reveals all guard patrol routes
- Ads shown between every 3rd level
