NGN4 GAME 26: MIND PALACE
════════════════════════════════════════════════
Memory Match Game

STORY: You are a Mental Architect constructing a Mind Palace. Match the memory fragments to strengthen your cognitive fortress.

GAMEPLAY:
- Flip 2 cards per turn. If they match, they stay revealed.
- Clear all pairs to complete each level.
- Use hints (costs 10 coins) to briefly peek at all cards.
- Build combos with consecutive matches for bonus rewards.

FEATURES:
- 20 themed levels with increasing difficulty
- Grid sizes from 4x3 to 6x6
- 4 card themes: Symbols, Colors, Numbers, NGN Characters
- Move counter, timer, star rating system
- Combo system with bonus rewards
- Hint system (costs coins)
- Star ratings based on moves vs par score
- Level progression with unlock system

REWARDS:
- +2 coins per match
- +20 coins combo bonus (3+ consecutive)
- +50 coins level clear
- +15 coins per star earned
- Hint costs 10 coins

CONTROLS:
- Click/Tap to flip cards
- Use Hint button to peek

AD INTEGRATION:
- Between levels (every other level)
- Rewarded ad for peek feature
