CHEF'S ARENA - NGN4 Game #39
Cooking / Restaurant Management Game

STORY:
Welcome to Chef's Arena - the ultimate cooking competition. Prepare dishes, serve customers, and become the Neon Culinary Champion.

CONTROLS:
Mouse - Click ingredients, stations, and serve dishes
Follow recipe order to prepare dishes correctly
Click customers to take orders

FEATURES:
- 30 recipes across 5 cuisines (American, Japanese, Italian, Mexican, Indian)
- Ingredient stations
- Cooking stations (Stove, Oven, Grill, Fryer)
- Timer per order (customer patience)
- Combo for fast service
- Upgrade kitchen equipment (faster cooking)
- Hire assistant chefs
- 15 levels (unlock new recipes)
- Tip system based on speed/accuracy
- Food burning mechanic

REWARDS:
Tips = coins
Level completion bonus
Recipe unlocks
Kitchen upgrades cost coins

AD INTEGRATION:
Rewarded ad between levels for instant ingredient restock
