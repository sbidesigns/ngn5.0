NGN4 Game 10: Court Legends
=============================
Genre: Basketball
Engine: Canvas 2D
Controls: Arrow keys to move, SPACE to shoot/block, A to sprint, S to steal

Story:
From street courts to the Neon League championship — prove you're the greatest to ever play.

Features:
- Shot meter system with power and arc timing
- 4 shot types: Layup, Midrange, Three-pointer, Dunk
- 4 quarters of 60 seconds each
- AI opponent with varying difficulty
- Tournament mode (5 rounds) and Quick Play
- Stat upgrade system (Accuracy, Speed, Power, Defense, Stamina)
- Steal and block mechanics
- Shot clock (24 seconds)
- Sprint with stamina management
- Swish/dunk visual effects
- Court with proper markings (three-point lines, center circle)
- NGN4 coin rewards and ad integration between tournament rounds

File: index.html, style.css, game.js
