NGN4 Game #18: Dead Signal
===========================
Genre: Top-Down Horror Survival
Story: You received a distress signal from a derelict space station. The rescue team never returned. Now you're trapped inside.

Features:
- 5 chapters with procedurally generated maps
- Flashlight with battery management
- Limited visibility (flashlight cone + ambient darkness)
- Item inventory (batteries, keycards, medkits, signal flares, documents)
- Creature AI with 4 states: patrol, alert, investigate, chase
- Creature speeds up over chapters
- Hiding mechanic (lockers, crates)
- Puzzle locks requiring keycards
- Sanity meter affecting visuals
- Jump scares on death
- 3 endings based on documents found and death count
- Documents collectible for lore
- Progressive difficulty

Controls:
- WASD: Move
- F: Toggle flashlight
- E: Interact (doors, hiding spots)
- 1-5: Use inventory item
- Tab: Open inventory

Rewards:
- +100 coins per chapter completed
- +50 coins per document found
- +50 bonus for no-death run
- +100-300 bonus for endings

Ad Integration:
- Interstitial ad between chapters
- Rewarded ad for full battery recharge
