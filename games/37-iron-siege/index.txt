IRON SIEGE - NGN4 Game #37
Tank Battle Game

STORY:
The Iron War has raged for decades. Command your tank battalion through 20 battlefields to end the conflict once and for all.

CONTROLS:
WASD - Move tank
Mouse - Aim turret
Click - Fire cannon

FEATURES:
- 6 tank types: Light, Medium, Heavy, Artillery, Scout, Super
- Destructible terrain (buildings)
- 20 missions across battlefields
- Enemy tanks with AI
- Base capture mechanic
- Ammo/reload system
- Terrain types: Open, Forest, Water, Buildings
- Friendly AI units
- Medal system per mission

REWARDS:
+15 coins per enemy tank
+100 coins per mission complete
Bonus for no losses
Tank upgrades cost coins

AD INTEGRATION:
Rewarded ad between missions for instant reload/repair
