NGN4 GAME 30: BRAIN VAULT
════════════════════════════════════════════════
Trivia/Quiz Game

STORY: The Brain Vault contains all knowledge. Prove your intellect to unlock its deepest chambers.

GAMEPLAY:
- Answer 15 multiple-choice questions per quiz round
- Choose difficulty: Easy (15s), Medium (10s), Hard (7s)
- Build streaks for bonus points
- Use lifelines when stuck
- Master categories for bonus rewards

FEATURES:
- 110+ questions across 10 categories:
  * Science (12), History (12), Gaming (12), Space (11)
  * Music (10), Movies (11), Tech (11), Nature (10)
  * Geography (10), Pop Culture (11)
- 3 difficulty modes with time limits
- Streak bonus system (consecutive correct = score multiplier)
- 3 lifelines: 50/50, Skip, Ask Audience
- 15-round quiz format
- Speed-based scoring (faster = more points)
- Category mastery system (80%+ = bonus 100 coins)
- Personal leaderboard (top 20 scores)
- Grade system: Genius/Expert/Skilled/Learner/Novice

SCORING:
- Base: 10 points + time bonus per correct answer
- Difficulty multiplier: Easy x1, Medium x1.5, Hard x2
- Streak multiplier: +10% per consecutive correct
- Max streak tracked

REWARDS:
- +10 coins per correct answer
- +5 coins per max streak level
- +50 coins quiz completion
- +100 coins for 80%+ category mastery
- Lifelines start free each round

CONTROLS:
- Click/Tap to select answer
- Use lifeline buttons when needed
- Timer auto-advances on timeout

AD INTEGRATION:
- Between quizzes (every 3rd quiz)
- Rewarded ad for extra lifeline

CATEGORIES:
🔬 Science - Physics, Chemistry, Biology
📜 History - World events and civilizations
🎮 Gaming - Video games and consoles
🚀 Space - Astronomy and the cosmos
🎵 Music - Instruments, genres, artists
🎬 Movies - Film trivia and directors
💻 Tech - Computers and technology
🌿 Nature - Animals, plants, Earth
🌍 Geography - Countries and landmarks
🎭 Pop Culture - Books, TV, superheroes
