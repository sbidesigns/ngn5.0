NGN4 Game #34: Fairway Fury
Genre: Sports / Mini Golf
Story: The Intergalactic Mini Golf Championship brings the best putters from across the cosmos. Can you sink the perfect round?
Features: 18 holes across 3 courses (Neon City, Crystal Cave, Sky Islands), obstacles (walls, water, sand, bumpers, wind, teleporters), par system, stroke counter, scorecard, 3-star rating, ball trail, hole-in-one detection
Controls: Click and drag on ball to aim, release to shoot
Rewards: Under par = coins, birdie +10, eagle +25, hole-in-one +100, course completion bonus
Ad: Rewarded ad for mulligan (redo last hole)
