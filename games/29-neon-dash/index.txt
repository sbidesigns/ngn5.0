NGN4 GAME 29: NEON DASH
════════════════════════════════════════════════
Endless Runner Game

STORY: Running through the digital highway at lightspeed. Dodge obstacles, collect data fragments, never stop.

GAMEPLAY:
- Auto-run, tap/click/space/up to jump (double-jump available)
- Swipe down/ArrowDown to slide under low beams
- Dodge obstacles: barriers, gaps, low beams, laser grids
- Collect coins for rewards
- Grab power-ups for special abilities

FEATURES:
- 5 vehicles with unique bonuses:
  * Runner (free), Hoverboard (100), Motorcycle (250), Sports Car (500), Jet (1000)
- Vehicle bonuses: Float, Speed boost, Shield, Fly
- 4 obstacle types with varied mechanics
- 4 power-ups: Magnet, Shield, 2x Coins, Mega Jump
- 10 progressive missions
- Increasing speed with distance
- Double-jump mechanic
- Particle effects and neon visuals
- City background with buildings and stars

REWARDS:
- +1 coin per coin collected (x2 with power-up)
- +10 coins per power-up collected
- Mission completion bonuses (30-120 coins)
- Vehicle upgrades cost coins

CONTROLS:
- Click/Tap/Space/ArrowUp: Jump
- ArrowDown/Swipe Down: Slide
- Double-jump available

AD INTEGRATION:
- Death screen: Rewarded ad for continue with shield
- Every 5 mission completions

MISSIONS:
1. Collect 50 coins in one run (+50)
2. Jump 30 times in one run (+40)
3. Survive 1000m (+60)
4. Slide 15 times (+30)
5. Collect 100 coins total (+80)
6. Complete 5 runs (+50)
7. Reach 500m (+70)
8. Jump 50 times (+50)
9. Collect 200 coins total (+120)
10. Reach 2000m (+100)
