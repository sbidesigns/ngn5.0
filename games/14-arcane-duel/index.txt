NGN4 Game Engine - Game #14
Title: Arcane Duel
Genre: Card Battle
Version: 1.0

STORY:
The Arcane Tournament gathers the realm's greatest duelists. Build your deck, summon creatures, cast spells — become the Archmage. Defeat all 8 opponents to claim the title.

GAMEPLAY:
- Card game vs AI opponents
- Each player starts with 30 HP
- Draw cards, spend mana to play cards
- Creatures attack each turn
- Reduce opponent HP to 0 to win

CARD SCHOOLS (40 total cards):
- FIRE (10): Direct damage, aggressive creatures
- ICE (10): Control, freeze effects, taunt
- NATURE (10): Healing, steady creatures, taunt
- SHADOW (10): Debuffs, drain, growth mechanics

RARITY:
- Common: Basic cards, easy to acquire
- Rare: Stronger effects
- Epic: Powerful unique effects
- Legendary: Game-changing cards

FEATURES:
- Deck builder (choose 20 from collection)
- Mana system (increases each turn, max 10)
- Creature cards with attack/health
- Spell cards with instant effects
- 8 AI opponents with themed decks
- Card packs purchasable with coins (100 coins = 3 cards)
- Opponent rewards (win to earn cards from their school)

CONTROLS:
Click cards in hand to play them
Click creatures on field to attack
End Turn button to pass turn

REWARDS:
+10 coins per win, +5 per loss
Card packs: 100 coins = 3 random cards (rarity weighted)
Defeat opponent: Earn card from their school + bonus coins

AD INTEGRATION:
- Rewarded ad for free card pack
- Ad between tournament rounds
