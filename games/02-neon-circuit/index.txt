# NGN4 Game: Neon Circuit
## 3D Racing

### Overview
The Neon Circuit is the most dangerous race in the galaxy. Win to earn your freedom from the Corporation's indentured pits.

### Gameplay
- 3D oval track racing using Babylon.js
- 3 AI opponents with varying skill levels
- 3 laps per race
- 5 tracks with increasing difficulty

### Controls
- W/↑: Accelerate
- S/↓: Brake
- A/← or D/→: Steer
- SPACE: Activate boost

### Features
- Speed boost pads on track
- Drift mechanics
- Car upgrades (Speed, Acceleration, Handling) purchasable with NGN coins
- Position tracking (1st-4th)
- Lap timer with best lap tracking
- Track unlocking system

### Tracks
1. Neon Oval (Easy) - Free
2. Twisted Ring (Medium) - 300 coins
3. Plasma Loop (Hard) - 600 coins
4. Void Circuit (Expert) - 1000 coins
5. Apex Gauntlet (Master) - 2000 coins

### Rewards
- 1st Place: +200 coins
- 2nd Place: +100 coins
- 3rd Place: +50 coins
- 4th Place: +25 coins
- Fast lap bonus: +50 coins (under 15s)
- Watch ad for double prize money

### Ad Integration
- Interstitial ads between races
- Rewarded ads on results screen for double coins

### Technical
- Babylon.js CDN for 3D rendering
- Neon glow effects with Babylon.js GlowLayer
- Elliptical track generation
- Chase camera system
- localStorage for upgrades and progress
