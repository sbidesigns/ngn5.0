NGN4 Game #16: GravShift
=======================
Genre: Physics Puzzle
Story: A malfunction shifted gravity across the research station. Navigate shifting gravitational fields to reach the emergency shuttle.

Features:
- 20 levels with increasing difficulty
- 4-direction gravity rotation (Arrow Keys or 1-4)
- Physics objects (boxes, balls) affected by gravity
- Hazards (spikes, lasers)
- Switches and doors puzzle mechanics
- Collectible data chips with star rating
- Full undo system (Z key) and restart (R key)
- Level select with progress tracking

Controls:
- Arrow Keys / 1-4: Rotate gravity direction
- Z: Undo last move
- R: Restart level

Rewards:
- +20 coins per level completed
- +10 coins per star earned (max 3 stars)
- +5 coins per data chip collected

Ad Integration:
- Interstitial ad every 5 levels
- Rewarded ad for skip level option
