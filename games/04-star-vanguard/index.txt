# NGN4 Game: Star Vanguard
## 3D Space Shooter

### Overview
Commander, the Void Fleet approaches. You are humanity's last defense at the Vanguard Line.

### Gameplay
- Forward-scrolling 3D space shooter using Babylon.js
- WASD to move, SPACE/Click to shoot, Q for special weapon
- Waves of enemies with boss battles every 5 waves
- 10 waves per sector, 5 sectors total

### Controls
- WASD: Move ship
- SPACE or Click: Shoot
- P: Pause
- Mobile: Drag to move, auto-fire enabled

### Enemy Types
1. Scout (Red) - Fast, low HP, 10 coins, 50 points
2. Cruiser (Orange) - Medium speed/HP, 25 coins, 150 points
3. Destroyer (Purple) - Slow, high HP, 50 coins, 300 points
4. Boss (every 5th wave) - 200 coins, 1000 points

### Power-ups
- Shield: Restore shield energy
- Rapid Fire: Increased fire rate for limited time
- Spread Shot: Triple projectile spread
- Health: Restore hull integrity
- Missile: Powerful projectile

### Ship Upgrades (Hangar)
- Damage: Increases projectile damage
- Fire Rate: Faster shooting
- Shield: More shield capacity
- Hull HP: More health points
- Speed: Faster movement

### Rewards
- 10 coins per scout kill
- 25 coins per cruiser kill
- 50 coins per destroyer kill
- 200 coins per boss kill
- +300 sector clear bonus (+100 per sector tier)
- Upgrades cost coins

### Ad Integration
- Interstitial ads between sectors
- Rewarded ad for revive on game over (restore full health/shield)

### Technical
- Babylon.js CDN for 3D rendering
- GlowLayer for neon effects
- Procedural enemy spawning
- Star field scrolling background
- localStorage for upgrades
